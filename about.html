About Us

Thermo Industrial Ltd (TIL), established in the year 2011, is one of the topmost manufacturers, and suppliers of a broad assortment of optimum quality Industrial Chemicals. Offered range of products consists of Caustic Soda Solution of varying concentrations of 15% to 50%, Sodium Hypochlorite solution 10% to 15% and Sodium Metabisulphite Solution 30%. Backed by the experience and knowledge of skilled professionals, we have been able to meet the precise needs of our valuable customers in the most efficient manner with Quality being our utmost priority which is maintained at all times. Offered range of Thermo products are available in various packaging from 25kg gallons to 45,000kg bulk tanker delivery and packaging unit. Moreover, in adherence to the set industry norms and regulation by using advanced processing tools, our professionals process the offered range of products.
